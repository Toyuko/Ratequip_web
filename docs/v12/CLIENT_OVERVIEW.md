# RateQuip — simple overview for clients

## What is RateQuip?

RateQuip is a marketplace and buying workspace for industrial equipment and services. It helps buyers find the right suppliers, run a fair quote process, and keep a clear record after the deal — from “we need this” through to “the machine is on site.”

## What problem does it solve?

Industrial buying is often messy: wrong-fit suppliers, unclear why someone was shortlisted, informal approvals, and paperwork that gets lost after award. RateQuip makes the path clearer and more accountable.

## How a typical project works

1. **Set up** — An AI-guided interview asks about your role, industry and how you operate. You confirm suggestions before they stick, then see trusted and relevant companies matched to your profile.  
2. **Describe the need** — Use shared equipment categories so everyone means the same thing.  
3. **See a shortlist** — Matches come with reasons, not a black box.  
4. **Approve and quote** — Internal approval, then RFQ, comparison, and award with a trail.  
5. **Keep the record** — Award can create an equipment record and store certificates safely.  
6. **Optional: read a long specification** — Paste a URS/RFQ; accept or reject what the system found. You see the credit cost first.  
7. **Optional (suppliers): turn a brochure into drafts** — Paste catalogue text, confirm the credit estimate, accept/reject product drafts. Nothing goes public until you publish.

## Promises we keep

- No hidden charges — you see usage/credit impact before you commit.  
- No silent AI decisions — consequential actions need human confirmation.  
- Company data stays within company boundaries.  
- Awards and approvals leave an auditable trail.

## Ready now vs coming later

**Ready to try:** setup, matching, purchase → RFQ → award, asset record, approvals, documents, specification reading, credit preview, supplier catalogue drafts.

**Later phases:** live OCR scanning of PDF brochures, broader marketplaces, finance/freight/labs, deep links into ERP/factory systems.
